📖 Our Story

Why PERA TRACK? I got tired of not knowing where my money went every month. Spreadsheets felt clunky, bank apps felt limited. I wanted something built exactly for my life — local categories, multiple profiles, real insights. So I built it.

Started in early 2026 as a personal project, PERA TRACK grew into something I actually use every day. The goal was always simple: see your money clearly, act on it deliberately.

🔐 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. PERA TRACK is committed to protecting your personal information.

What We Collect

PERA TRACK stores your financial entries, profile names, PINs (not transmitted in plain text), and preference settings.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is stored securely via Supabase and is used solely to power your personal finance dashboard. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data.

Your Rights

You may delete your profile and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete This Profile).

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at help@peratrack.app.
